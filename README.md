# fopnl-umkm
Front-of-Pack Nutrition Labels (FOPNLs) are simplified nutrition information placed on the front side of food packaging, designed to help consumers quickly understand the nutritional quality of a product. While large-scale industries have started implementing FOPNLs, Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) often face challenges in adopting this system due to limited resources, lack of knowledge, and the absence of accessible digital tools.  Digitalization provides opportunities for SMEs to develop standardized FOPNLs with lower costs and wider reach. Through mobile applications, QR codes, and cloud-based databases, SMEs can integrate nutrition labeling more efficiently, enhancing consumer trust and competitiveness in both domestic and global markets.
